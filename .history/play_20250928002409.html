My Portfolio

I’ve always been drawn to details, whether that’s the alignment of a button or the shadow falling across a street. Like good design, good photography starts with asking: “What do I want someone to feel when they see this?” I see this way of seeing as something I bring into everything I do, whether it’s design, code, or content. It’s helped me tell more honest stories, build more meaningful experiences, and notice the small things that make a project not just work, but feel right.
